Travel Directions

Visa information for foreign travelers

Please apply for your visas as soon as possible. Information on visas for meeting organizers and participants is available at: http://national-academies.org/visas

Letter of invitation

Individuals requiring letters of invitation to obtain travel visas must be listed in the program to receive an invitation letter. Individuals requiring letters of invitation to obtain travel visas to present their papers need to send an e-mail to Conference Staff at InviteLetter@asilomarssc.org. Please, include the paper number, author(s), and title in your correspondence. All letters of invitation will be sent by e-mail (as PDF attachments), airmail or faxed. Please allow ample time for processing requests. The Conference staff is not able to contact U.S. Embassies in support of an individual attempting to gain entry to attend the conference. We recommend that you start your visa application process as soon as you have been notified that your paper has been accepted, as the visa application process can be lengthy. Cancellations after the registration cutoff will result in a cancellation fee.

Directions to the Asilomar Hotel and Conference Grounds, Pacific Grove

If you are coming from San Francisco, or San Jose Airport (by car)
  1. take HW 101 south to 156 west.
  2. from 156 west take HW1 south.
  3. stay on HW1 south until you reach the SECOND HW68 exit (HW68 West), the exit signs will say Asilomar-Pacific Grove-Pebble Beach).
  4. At the exit, turn RIGHT at the first traffic light, this will be HW68 West. Note that the 1st and 2nd HW68 exit are a few miles apart. The exit road will be quite windy for a few miles as you go down to sea level, you'll pass the hospital on your right as you go down to sea level. HW68 will turn into Forest Avenue, get into the left lane, and follow the signs for HW68 (turn left on Sunset Drive).
  5. You'll see signs for Asilomar Center a few miles down.
If you are coming from San Francisco/San Jose airport (Shuttle Service Option available ???)

The Monterey/Salinas airbus is a direct airport-to-airport bus which picks up passengers from various terminals in San Francisco & San Jose airports. It takes about 1.25 hours between San Jose airport and Monterey, and about 2 hours between the San Francisco airport and Monterey. Click here for further details and schedule. The Phone number is (831)-373-7777. It is possible to arrange for direct drop-off at the Conference Grounds with advance reservations (this option is highly recommended for travelers as it will greatly simplify your trip). One-way fare is $45.00 (as of 01/09). Reservations are recommended.

If you are coming from the Monterey Airport (by car)
  1. Exit airport and take HW68 towards Monterey. (you should make a right turn at the first traffic light). At the rental car counter, ask for specific directions to connect to HW68 from the airport, as they are two ways to leave the airport....
  2. take HW1 south, and stay on it until you see exit for HW68 - Pebble Beach, Asilomar exit (HW68 West).
  3. At the exit, turn RIGHT at the first traffic light, this will be HW68 West. The exit road will be quite windy for a few miles as you go down to sea level, you'll pass the hospital on your right as you go down to sea level. HW68 will turn into Forest Avenue, get into the left lane, and follow the signs for HW68 (turn left on Sunset Drive).
  4. You'll see signs for Asilomar Center a few miles down.
If you are coming from Monterey Airport (Public transportation option)

Very limited bus service is provided by Monterey-Salinas Public Transit from the Monterey airport to the Asilomar Conference Grounds located at 800 Asilomar Blv, Pacific Grove. Please see www.mst.org for current routes and schedules.Lines 7, 56, and 93 serve the airport with limited availability.

If you are coming from Monterey Airport ( Shuttle service option)

Shuttle service between the Monterey Airport and Asilomar Conference Grounds or local hotels is provided by advance reservation with the Monterey/Salinas Airbus. Click here for pricing and further details. The Phone number is (831)-373-7777.

If you're coming from Monterey Airport (shared rides)

Check with Lift and Uber for their availability in serving the Monterey airport.

If you want to travel to Monterey and the rest of the peninsula from/to the Asilomar Conference Grounds by public transportation

Monterey Salinas Transit (MST) Line 1offers a convenient and low cost way to connect Asilomar conference grounds with the rest of the Peninsula (downtown PG and Monterey, Monterey Bay Aquarium, Carmel, etc...). Please see www.mst.org for further details on routes, schedules and costs.
