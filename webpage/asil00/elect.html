2000 Electronic submission

Please follow the following instructions if you wish to submit electronic submissions:

  • Plain text file format submission(s) are accepted by e-mail to submitAS@ece.nps.navy.mil.

  • Please, use a plain text file format, as no other format will be accepted at this address.

  • The body of your e-mail message should contain the following information in the order indicated below (please, do not use attachements):
    1. Paper title,
    2. Up to three keywords,
    3. Authors and affiliations,
    4. Point of contact (POC) to whom all correspondence will be sent,
    5. POC full mailing address, phone, fax, and e-mail address.
    6. Paper abstract, not to exceed 100 words.
    7. Extended summary, not to exceed 1000 words.
    8. Tables may be added, provided that they are in plain text format too.

PLEASE, READ CAREFULLY

A. Please, do not send .tex, .ps or any other format except plain text file format, as we do not have the capability to accomodate them at this address, and such submissions will rejected without review. Should your submission(s) contain graphic files, please send your submission(s) via regular mail or fed-ex, as specified in the call for papers.

B. Only COMPLETE electronic submissions (.i.e., with both abstracts AND summary) received before the deadline will be logged in. We will NOT ACCEPT partial electronic submissions (i.e., submissions where the abstract is sent electronically, and the summary is sent separately via regular mail).

C. Please, DO NOT e-mail electronic submissions to asilomar@ece.nps.navy.mil , as they will be rejected without review. Thank you.
