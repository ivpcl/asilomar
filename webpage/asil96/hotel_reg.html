               !!!!!!IMPORTANT NOTES, PLEASE READ CAREFULLY!!!!!!!

1. CANCELLATION POLICY: Cancellations received in writing after 10/1/96 is subject
 to $25.00 cancellation fee.  Cancellation notice after 10/15/96 is subject to
 forfeiture of all fees if space is not sold.  In case of cancellation on day of
 arrival or early departure, all fees forfeited.
2.All sleeping and meeting rooms are non smoking. Note: there are no TV's or phones
 in sleeping accommodations.  In-house and public phones available throughout the
 Grounds.
3. Checks drawn on any non-US banks MUST include a $25.00 processing fee.  Any
 additional processing charge is the conferee's responsibility.
4. Prices shown  are for one person, including housing, tax, and meals (except 
dinner on Monday night).  If accompanied by spouse, double the rate.
5. Meal Service is "American Plan", i.e., dinner each night you are staying at
 Asilomar except Monday, and breakfast and lunch the following day.  However, 
 special arrangements may be made for those who wish to eat at Asilomar on Monday 
 evening.  Please, indicate whether you would be interested in eating at Asilomar 
 on Monday night by checking the appropriate box on this form.
6. We cannot guarantee room availability for reservations received after 10/5/96.  
The conference is charged 3 full days for all rooms, hence priority to room 
assignments will be given to 3-day registrants.
7. Check-in for rooms at the conference desk is 3pm.  Check-out time is 12 noon.
8. For infants 2 and under crib use is not charged.  Children under 18 allowed in 
the charged room at reduced rate (call Asilomar for information).
9. On Sunday night the conference will host a welcoming reception from 7:30pm to 
9pm. Location to be announced in the final program.
10. Monday night a no-host cocktail party will be held at the Officer's and Faculty 
Club of the Naval Postgraduate School in lieu of dinner at the Asilomar.  Hors 
d'oeuvres will be served.
