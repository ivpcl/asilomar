Final Paper Submission & Publication Information

    Note that the Asilomar Conference has a long standing policy to restrict publication in the Conference Proceedings only to papers which have been presented by one of the authors of the paper(s), and that author be registered at the Conference for the paper to be published. NO exceptions are made to this rule. No late papers will be accepted. Accepted papers turned in and not presented as requested will be pulled from the publication stack.

Authors must follow the following three steps to insure publication of their paper(s):

  3. Submit two copies of the completed manuscript and the copyright form at the conference registration desk (Deadline: 12:00pm PST on Wednesday, November 6, 2002).
