Call for Special Sessions

deadline: January 15, 2002

 

    Prospective organizers are invited to submit proposals for special sessions to Dr. Louis Scharf at scharf@engr.colostate.edu or Dr. Ben Friedlander at friedlan@cse.ucsc.edu by January 15, 2002. Proposals must include a topical title, rationale, session outline, contact information, and a description of how the session will be organized. Format for special sessions will be similar to that of the 2001 conference, and the interested organizers are referred to the conference website for further information regarding the technical program format.
