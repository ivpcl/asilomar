Submission Format Information:

  1. Submissions should include a 50 to 100 word abstract and an extended summary (500 to 1000 words plus figures). 

  2. Submissions must include the paper title, each author's name and affiliation, and the technical area(s) with number(s) from the above list, where the paper falls. In addition, each submission should include the name, full address, telephone/fax numbers, and e-mail address of the Point of Contact for the paper. 

  3. No more than four submissions total are allowed per contributor, as author or co-author. Please, note that the limitation applies to the combination of any type of submission (i.e., invited or regular). Any submission where at least one of the author appears on more than four submissions will be rejected without review.

  4. All submissions must be received by June 3, 2002. 

  5. Notifications of acceptance will be mailed by late August 2002, and author information will sent by mid September 2002. 

  6. Full papers are due at the Conference, and will be published in the spring of 2003. Please note: Papers must be presented by one of the authors of the papers, and that author be registered at the Conference for the paper to be published.  NO exceptions are made to this rule. Accepted papers turned in and not presented as requested will be pulled from the publication stack.

  7. All technical questions should be directed to the Technical Program Chair, Dr. Louis Scharf, e-mail: scharf@engr.colostate.edu, or the General Chair: Dr. Ben Friedlander, e-mail: friedlan@cse.ucsc.edu.
