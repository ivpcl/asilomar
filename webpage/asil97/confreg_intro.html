Technical Conference Registration Information
  • Conference Registration Form (.PDF format)

  • Regitration fees must be remitted in U.S. dollars, made out to: SS & C Corporation

  • Accepted modes of payment:
    Payment for the conference registration fees can be made by:
    • checks drawn on U.S. banks only,
    • traveller's checks (in U.S. currency),
    • U.S. bank draft (i.e., bank check made out to the SS & C Corporation drawn on a U.S. bank),
    • cash.
    Please, note: credit card, purchase order, or check drawnn on foreign banks not accepted. No exceptions made.
  • Please note:
    1. We do not accept faxed registrations. Faxed registrations or registrations sent without accompanying payment will be disregarded.
    2. Prospective attendees who sent registrations without payment will need to re-register upon arrival and will need to pay the late registration fee.
