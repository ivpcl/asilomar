Overall Conference Program

Scheduling Details:

  • Presentation length:
    1. Oral Presentations:
      Paper presentation are scheduled every 25mn for all oral presentation, except for sessions MA1 and MA5. Authors will be allowed 20mn for oral presentation, and 5mn will be reserved for questions.
      Note A: Papers scheduled for presentation in sessions MA1 and MA5 are scheduled every 20mn. Authors in those two sessions will be allowed 15mn for oral presentation and 5mn will be reserved for questions.
    2. Poster Presentations:
      Poster presentations last 1 hour and 30mn. Refer to the schedule below for specific times.
