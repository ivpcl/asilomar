1995 Asilomar Conference on Signals, Systems, and Computers (electronic information)

CALL FOR PAPERS

Before June 1, 1995 authors are invited to submit papers in the following general areas: 

  1. Multidimensional Signal &        9. Time Frequency Representations
     Image Processing                    
  2. Linear & Nonlinear Digital      10. Analog & Digital VLSI Design
     Signal Processing
  3. Adaptive Signal Processing      11. DSP Hardware Implementation
  4. Sensor Array Processing         12. Wavelets and Filter Banks
  5. Speech Processing               13. Higher-Order Statistics
  6. Communications                  14. Video Technology
  7. Radar and Sonar                 15. Neural Networks
  8. Biomedical                      16. Others
Two copies of a 50 to 100 word abstract and an extended summary (500 to 1000 words plus figures) should be submitted. The extended summary will be evaluated for its significance and originality. Abstracts must be headed by the paper title, each author's name, affiliation, full address, telephone/fax number, e-mail address and the technical areas(s) with number(s) from the above list, where the paper falls. Abstracts should be suitable for printing in an Abstract Book which will be available at the Conference. NO MORE THAN FOUR SUBMISSIONS PER CONTRIBUTOR, AS AUTHOR OR CO-AUTHOR, ARE ALLOWED. ALL SUBMISSIONS MUST BE RECEIVED BY JUNE 1, 1995. Notifications of acceptance will be mailed by the end of July 1995, and author kits will be sent by mid September 1995. Full papers will be due at the Conference, and published in the spring of 1996. ALL TECHNICAL QUESTIONS SHOULD BE DIRECTED TO THE TECHNICAL PROGRAM CHAIRMAN, DR. MICHAEL A. SODERSTRAND at dsplab@ece.ucdavis.edu, phone (916) 752-2669, fax (916) 752-8428.

Regular mail submissions should be sent to:

Asilomar Conference Technical Program Chairman
c/o ECE Department, Code EC
Naval Postgraduate School

Monterey, CA 93943-5121

ADDITIONAL INSTRUCTIONS:

1. REGULAR MAIL SUBMISSIONS:

In addition to the material described above, authors should include with their submission a separate page containing a camera ready copy of the abstract headed by the paper title, and each author's name and affiliation. The typeface must be clear and legible, and all information on this page must fit within a 2.5" high by 6.5" wide area for printing in the Abstract book.

2.ELECTRONIC SUBMISSIONS:

Only one copy of the material need to be e-mailed to asilomar@ece.nps.navy.mil . Only plain text format will be accepted.

FTP SITE INFORMATION: Registration and Advance Program Information will be available after 7/15/95 at the anonymous ftp site ftp.nps.navy.mil in directory /pub/ece/asilomar.
