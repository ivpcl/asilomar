Post Conference Information 

  • 2001 Conference Proceedings: 
    Proceedings are expected to be shipped in February 2002, Please check again around 1/15/02 for more precise information. For
    printed copies: US/Canada shipping is done 1st class. Overseas shipping is done via surface mail, which takes longer. For CD
    proceedings: all shipping is by 1st class. 

  • Lost copies instructions:


    • If you don't receive your proceedings by March 30th for US addresses we will send you a duplicate. Overseas shipments are sent via
    surface mail (which take quite a lot longer unfortunately). For overseas shipments, please wait until June 1st before contacting us.

    • Conferences prior to year 2000: Note proceedings are mailed only to full registrants (student registrants do not get a copy of the
      proceedings). 

    • Conferences in 2000 and later: CD proceedings are mailed to all registrants. 

      To verify eligibility and get a duplicate copy, please mail a copy of the conference registration receipt to: 
      Prof. M. P. Fargues

      Naval Postgraduate School 
      Monterey, CA 93943.
