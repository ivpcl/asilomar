Note to authors submitting papers:

  • NO more than 4 submissions are allowed per contributor, as author or co-author.

  • At least one author of the paper must be physically present at the conference and present the paper at the conference for the paper to be included in the proceedings. No exception is made to this policy.

  • The author presenting the paper at the conference must be registered for the conference.
