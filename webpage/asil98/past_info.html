1997 Conference information

  • 1997 Proceedings were mailed 4/28/98 by IEEE, regular mail for US addresses (expect 5 day delivery), surface mail for overseas delivery. Airmail is used for US mailings, surface mail is used for overseas mailings.

  • Full registrants receive one copy of the Proceedings, and one copy of the Proceedings on CD-Rom. Student registrants do not receive copies of the Proceedings

  • If you have not received your proceedings by the end of May (for US), or June (for overseas), please contact the Conference Registrar: Ms. Sue Netzorg, (408) 656-2082, e-mail: snetzorg@nps.navy.mil. (e-mail preferred). You will be asked to mail or fax a copy of your 1997 registration form as registration proof.

  • We have a limited number of 1997 proceedings for sale. Please contact Ms. Sue Netzorg, (408) 656-2082, e-mail: snetzorg@nps.navy.mil, if you are interested.
