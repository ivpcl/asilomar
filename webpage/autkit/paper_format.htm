Papers Presentation Format

  1. Papers in sessions with numeric "8" are to be presented in a poster format. Thus, MA8a, MA8b, MP8a, TA8a, TA8b, TP8a, TP8b, WA8 are to be presented in a poster format.
    All other papers are to be presented by oral presentation 20mn long (5mn questions allowed).

  2. Presentation Schedule:
    Paper identifiers consist of 2 letters, one number, and one optional letter. the first two letters identify the day of presentation, the number the session number, and the last letter identifies split sessions.

    • MA: Monday Morning
    • MP: Monday afternoon
    • TA: Tuesday morning
    • TP: Tuesday afternoon
    • WA: Wednesday morning
